Lester Test

IF YOU ARE HAVING AN EMERGENCY, SEEK HELP IMMEDIATELY.

The Lester Test is a test designed to measure anxiety about the subject of death and opinions related to death. It is not meant to be a diagnostic tool but can prove insightful for average people.



The test works as follows:

  • The following statements will present opinions of death. Check whether you agree (A) or disagree (D) with that statement.
  • Each statement has a value. The values of the statements you agreed with will be added together and divided by the number of statements you agreed with.
  • The result of the math will rest between the values of two statements. Those two statements will best represent your views on death. For example, if statement 5 has a value of 5.0, and statement 6 has a value of 5.51, and you scored 5.43, then statements 5 and 6 are best representative of your views.
